Signal Information
Signal Logic:
  • BUY: RSI < oversold AND price > SMA AND MACD bullish
  • SELL: RSI > overbought OR price < SMA OR MACD bearish
  • HOLD: No clear signal
Position Sizing:
  • Based on ATR (Average True Range) for volatility
  • Stop loss set at Entry - (ATR × multiplier)
  • Position size = Risk Amount ÷ (Entry - Stop Loss)
  • Take profit set at Entry + 2× (Entry - Stop Loss)
